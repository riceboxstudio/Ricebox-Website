How to Build an Arcade

We designed a manual on how to build an arcade as a result of graphic designers building an arcade from scratch and learning the fundamentals of product design along the way with the help of our 3D designer friend, Kelvin Yuen. We decided to document our process and newly acquired knowledge in the format of a foldable manual.
