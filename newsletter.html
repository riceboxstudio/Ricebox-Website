Ricebox Newsletter

The Ricebox Studio Newsletter, sent out on a monthly basis, is all about tech, education, design and activism! We started this newsletter in hopes to create a platform to share exciting finds in the art, design and tech industry and to connect more with creatives in these fields!

Every month we take new submissions around:
-Tech & Education
-Tech & Activism
-Tech & Design

Just send us a quick 1-2 sentence blurb, an image or link for a chance to feature!

Don't forget to subscribe to our newsletter and also check out our past issues!
