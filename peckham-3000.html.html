Peckham 3000

In the “What’s Your Story?” program, we taught the basics of AR through storytelling and racial equality. It consisted of a Briefing Talk, AR Technical Workshop, 3-day Hackathon and ended with a Live Brief with Sisterhood, where students applied their new AR techniques in real-time to bring the book Find Me Among Them to life! A big round of applause to Aneta Sobczuk, Marie Schaller, Siana Bezuhanova and Mezhabin Maleque who joined us for the full programme!

The “What’s Your Story?” program would not have happened without the collaboration of Sisterhood, Artivive and UAL Arts’ SU! Thank you so much!
