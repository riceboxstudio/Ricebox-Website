Techbox

Techbox is a series of guidebooks and hackathons encouraging the use of creative technology for social good, through not only learning how to use various tech tools, but also through learning design thinking skills. We have had the opportunity to run 6 workshops since August 2020, ranging from an 1 hour workshop to a 3 day Hackathon with 600+ sign ups! Please contact us if you would like us to run a workshop for you!
- Spark Activism with AR Instagram Filters with UAL Summer Sessions - Augmented Activism with UAL BA Graphic Design - AR & Activism Hackathon with UAL: CSM - Tech & Activism at the Tech for All Conference - Augmented Activism with UAL MA Graphic Design Communication - An Intro to Augmented Reality, Storytelling & Activism with Daisie
