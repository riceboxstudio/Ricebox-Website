The Whitewash Project

The whitewash project focuses on the gaps in western education. Through omitting crucial steps of our own history, the western educational system has completely failed both its students and its teachers. This project revisits and allows the audience to explore the idea of a ‘single’ history, the infamous quote ‘history is written by the victors’ and how this concept has shaped our educational system.
